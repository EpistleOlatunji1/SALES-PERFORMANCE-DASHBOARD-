# Project Overview

This project presents an enterprise sales performance analytics dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel to support revenue monitoring, growth analysis, and strategic business decision-making. The dashboard consolidates sales transaction data into interactive key performance indicators (KPIs) that provide real-time insights into revenue trends, product performance, regional growth patterns, and sales efficiency metrics.

The system is designed to support financial planning, managerial accounting, and strategic revenue optimization by enabling executives and managers to evaluate business performance, detect underperforming segments, and prioritize growth opportunities.

# Key Features

Automated revenue aggregation and growth trend analysis

Product and region-level sales performance segmentation

Interactive KPIs for revenue, volume, and contribution margins

Dynamic pivot tables and slicers for managerial decision support

Visual trend analysis and comparative performance benchmarking

# Applications

Corporate financial performance monitoring

Revenue forecasting and budget planning

Sales strategy optimization

Management reporting and executive dashboards

# Why This Matters

Effective financial decision-making depends on clear, timely performance analytics.
This dashboard demonstrates applied enterprise analytics capabilities that support financial planning, internal controls, and revenue optimization, aligning directly with financial systems efficiency and economic productivity.
